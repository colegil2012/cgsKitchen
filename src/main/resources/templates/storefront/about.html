About El Conejo

Celtech Kitchen was founded in 2026 as part of a Greater Project, Celtech General Store. Both were created with a single goal in mind: To Connect Kentucky Farmers with Kentucky Citizens in a meaningful and a mutually beneficial way.

Everything prepped by Celtech is sourced within 50 miles of Louisville, if you want to know what's in, or sprayed on your food, you can drive out and ask the farmers themselves, that's how I started buying from them.

With these ingredients we've derived a list of modernized Celtic, Irish, Wales, and Cornish recipes, and we're always cooking up new ones to add to the menu.

We rotate locations through the week. Check the home page or follow us on Instagram for today's spot.
